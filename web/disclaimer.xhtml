Cranachan - Data usage disclaimer

The majority of barley lines used for producing the datasets available are unpublished. Publication of the full dataset is in the planning stages and likely to occur in the first half of 2019. Until publication of the full dataset, publication of genotypes from unpublished lines is not permitted.

Of the 1,336 lines used, there are 275 published lines listed in the spreadsheet downloadable from here. These formed part of two publications which are listed in the spreadsheet. Publication of genotypes from these lines is permitted, but the source publication should be cited in all cases.
